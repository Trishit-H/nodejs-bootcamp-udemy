Looking for Alaska

John Green

Young Adult, Fiction

March 3, 2005

Miles Halter, a teenager obsessed with famous last words, leaves his safe and uninteresting life in Florida to attend Culver Creek Preparatory School in Alabama. There, he meets Alaska Young, a beautiful, enigmatic, and self-destructive girl who pulls him into her labyrinth of emotions. As Miles, nicknamed Pudge, forms deep friendships, he is drawn to Alaskaâ€™s rebellious spirit and troubled past. The novel explores the complexities of adolescence, from the thrill of first love to the pain of loss. After a tragic accident, Miles and his friends are left to piece together Alaska's life and confront the profound questions she left behind. 'Looking for Alaska' is a poignant exploration of life, death, and the impact one person can have on another, capturing the turbulence of growing up and the search for meaning in a seemingly chaotic world.
